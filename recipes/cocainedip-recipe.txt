Ingredients:
 - 1 pack of cream cheese
 - 1 Jalepeno
 - 1 can of rotel
 - 1 lb of breakfast sausage

Directions:
 1. Brown sausage in a frying pan over medium heat
 
 2. Drain the grease out of the frying pan

 3. Dice up Jalepeno and add to frying pan
 
 4. Lower heat and add rotel and cream cheese

 5. Mix until uniform
