When would you want to use a remote repository rather than keeping all of your work local?
When you have others collaborating on your project.
Why might you want to always pull changes manually rather than having Git automaticlly stay up-to-date with your remote repository?
That way you always know the status of your repository.
Describe the differences between forks, clones, and branches.  When would you use one instead of another?
Forks are when you make a copy of someone elses repository. Clones are when you copy a repository from the web to your computer. Branches are wehen you work on a local copy of a local branch.
